Things to do In Paraguay

This is Iguaza Falls
This is Iguaza Falls located on the borders of Paraguay, Argentina, and Brazil. In paraguay, the city a person could stay at is knownas Cuidad del Este. This is eco reserva mbatoví
This is eco reserva mbatoví located south of the ciy of Acunsion. It is full of rare flora and fauna such as the delicate fern known as Chachi which is close to extinction. There are many activities to be done such as hiking along sheer cliff faces, ziplining across 105m of rope, and bungee jumping off four of the bridges.
