WELCOME TO YBYCUI NATIONAL PARK


The Ybycui National Park is a protected area that contains a dense rainforest that was once part of the Atlantic rainforest. It contains steep hills with a relatively small area of 400 meters when compared to other parks. Although small on the surface, it is a diverse ecosystem with plenty of different areas to explore.

At the park there is an old iron foundry that has been turned into a museum. It was originally used to forge weapons during wars but now instead shows off the old weaponry it crafted as well as other pieces of historice importance.

There are also many trails a person can hike and explore while being able to see the beautiful scenery such as waterfalls and raging rivers.
