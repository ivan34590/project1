WELCOME TO ASUNCION



Asuncion is the capital of Paraguay as well as being the largest city there. It has an approximate population of 530,000 people. It is located on the eastern bank of the Paraguay river. Asuncion is one of the oldest known cities in South America and is known as the "Mother of Cities. It can have an incredibly hot climate with average temperatures in summer being above 90 everyday. With such a largest and diverse city, there is much to do."

The Shopping del Sol is the largest shopping mall in Paraguay. It contains hundreds of shops from expensive luxury brands such as Gucci to more affordable brands such as Nike. The surrounding area is teeming with wonderful restaurants and smaller stores selling daily needs to more tourist centered items.

Alhtough Asuncsion is not just a place for shopping. It contains many historic sites and museums one can explore if that is more what a person is looking for. The Panteon Nacional de los Heroes is one of these places. It is a beautiful landmark honoring soldiers lost in the war.
